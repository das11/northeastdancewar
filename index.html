Why us

NorthEast Dance War

We look at events as part of a larger internal and external communication process to deliver specific results. Briefs are evaluated and macro dimension is added to the project. Newness, creativity, impact, brandperception, communication and campaign perspectives are key parameters within which ideas are engineered into events.

North-east dance war 2019 aims to support young talents in the society to create strong statements and to teach the value of appreciation towards nurturing skills like dance and an overall quality of life. To showcase our culture, heritage, history & enchanted frontiers to the whole world through the power of broadcasting. We take it as an opportunity to improve the sustainibility and recognition of the community.

It is a great platform for young talents from all over the assam region to promote themselves, voice their opinions, get involved, network and at the same time fulfil their goals
